SHOPIFY

A HUB FOR BUYING AND SELLING OF PRODUCTS

INTRODUCTION

SHOPIFY is a web application for local community with a platform to help them buy, sell, and find something and address needs across many categories such as Clothing, Cars and Bikes, Mobiles, Real Estate, Furniture, TVs & electronics & Pets etc.

OUR GOALS

The overall architecture goals of the system are to provide a highly available and scalable online application for everybody where people can exchange goods and services..

Benefits Using SHOPIFY

Seller free and easy posting of their Advertisements.

The cost-effectiveness it bring to the business.

Great selection of classifies ads under different major categories.

Direct connection between buyer and seller.

Scope Of This Application

The scope of this Application is spread across all over the world. It is a unique platform to buy or sell first or second hand products. People can buy the products according to their needs even real estate property is also available.
